About Us

Freshiance was founded on May 25th 2018 with the goal of making eSports on mobile more entertaining and more popular than ever. Since then we are only doing more to expand our horizons and truly test our capability when it comes to Esports Competitive Event Organizing and Management.

When we began our journey, we chose an extremely niche market - Mobile Esports, back in early 2018 Mobile Esports was almost unheard of, we started our journey in the game Critical Ops as Community Tournament Organizers under Claxon Tournaments and went onto directly work with the publishers of Critical Ops - Critical Force Entertainment.

Later in 2020, we also entered the Call of Duty Mobile Scene.

Our Mission

To build an Esports Culture, we believe Esports provides the same kind of enertainment a sport does and hence work with a mission of making it as relevant as mainstream sports.
